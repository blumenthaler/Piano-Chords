Welcome to Piano Chords!

Instructions:

  • Click a key on the piano or press the corresponding key on the keyboard to hear a note.

  • To start learning about chords, select Chord Mode, a Chord Type, and play the root (starting) note to hear the chord and see note names.

  • (For Major Triad, click/press C to hear a C Major Triad)

  • If you are a beginner, I would start with Major Triad, Minor Triad, Diminished Triad, and Augmented Triad, before moving on to Seven Chords.
